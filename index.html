When talented, innovative people come together, amazing things can happen.

Whether you're a designer or a developer, a copywriter or a coder, working in tech or at a creative agency, Goodby Silverstein & Partners invites you to join us for a hackathon event we're calling Hack SF.

Bridge Logo Dark

How It Works

When

On Saturday and Sunday, January 25026, Hack SF teams will have 24 hours to come up with ideas to improve life in San Francisco.

Hack

Ideas may range from transportation fixes to apps to beautification projects to business ideas that will make the city more livable. Really, it's up to you.

We're confident that putting a bunch of creative people together for a weekend will lead to some innovative solutions.

We'll also have a bunch of different APIs and databases you can work with from the city of San Francisco's Open Data Portal.

You don't need to have an idea or team formed before you come to the event. Part of the fun of a hackathon is meeting other people and coming up with your own idea during the event.

An idea by itself lives only in the mind of an individual, but an idea shared among many can become reality.

Prizes

We're inviting five of the city's top creative and technical leaders to be a part of our judging panel. Teams will have 3 minutes each to present their ideas and their demos to the judges and the audience. We'll have cash prizes for the first, second, and third-place winners.

Hack SF Schedule

Saturday, January 25 - Day 1

  • 12:30 p.m.: Registration opens (people meet and form teams)
  • 1:30 p.m.: Hacking kickoff
  • 2:00 p.m.–7:00 p.m.: API workshops by technology providers
  • 7:00 p.m.: Dinner served
  • 8:00 p.m.–midnight: Hacking continues—teams can stay through the night or come back the next morning (drinks and snacks available)

Sunday, January 26 - Day 2

  • 7:00 a.m.: Breakfast served
  • 9:30 a.m.: Hacking concludes, and teams submit their hacks to the judges
  • 11:00 a.m.: Team presentations begin
  • 2:00 p.m.: Hackathon concludes with prizes awarded by judges
